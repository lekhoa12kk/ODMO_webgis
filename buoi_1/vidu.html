Explore the impacts of drought with the updated Drought Aware App The Drought Aware App provide insights on drought affected areas in the U.S., tracking the changing intensities of drought over time. The app integrates data layers in ArcGIS Living Atlas of the World to tabulate the population experiencing the effects of droughts, and the potential impacts to crop sales, agricultural labor, and streamflow.
